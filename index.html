Hello!

Hello! Welcome to Wellness Explorers! We are a McMaster medical-student run initiative based in Hamilton, Ontario.

Our goal is to ensure equal access to the outdoors for all school-aged children in Hamilton, and encourage children to take control of their own wellbeing.

Our program is running for the first time in 2024. We hope to see you sometime this summer!

We are constantly adding new content to our website – please check back regularly for updates.
